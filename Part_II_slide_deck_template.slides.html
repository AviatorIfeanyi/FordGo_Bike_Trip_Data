Investigation Overview¶

This data analysis investigation aims to get some insights about individual bike sharing trips. In this analysis, i aim to understand when and where are most trips taken, and what kind of characteristics that are worth studying in the dataset.

Dataset Overview¶

This data set includes information about individual bike rides made in a bike-sharing system in the San Francisco Bay area.¶

Subscribers vs Customers¶

Users who uses the bike sharing service are more of subscribers than customers¶

Bike rides per hour¶

The hours of 8a.m and 5p.m (17:00) are the peak hours for the day. This could be because the users are commuters who use the bike service in the morning while going to work and at night when coming back from work¶
